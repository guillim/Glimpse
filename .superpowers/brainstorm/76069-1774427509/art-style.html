Character Art Style

What pixel art style for the Pokemon characters? This affects how we render them and how much detail we can show for status indicators.

~16x16 sprites

A. Game Boy Style

Tiny 16x16 sprites like Gen 1/2 Pokemon. Very retro, fits many characters on screen. Status icons would be small overlays.

~32-48px sprites

B. GBA / Gen 3 Style

Medium 32-48px sprites like Ruby/Sapphire. Good balance of detail and density. Room for expressive status indicators (book, speech bubble, zzz).

~64-96px sprites

C. DS / Modern Pixel

Larger 64-96px sprites with more detail. Fewer characters fit on screen but each one is very expressive. Status indicators are full mini-icons.

Consider: with many Claude sessions open, smaller sprites scale better. With fewer (2-5), larger sprites look better.
