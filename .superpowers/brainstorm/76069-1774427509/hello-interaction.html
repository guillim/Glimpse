Hello Interaction

When the user's gaze lands on a character, it says hello. How should this work?

A

Gaze Dwell (look for ~1 second)

Character reacts only after you've been looking at it for about 1 second. Prevents accidental triggers when scanning across the screen. A small "noticing" animation plays first (character perks up), then the hello bubble appears above.

B

Nearest Character Reacts

The character closest to your gaze point always reacts — instantly. Fast and responsive, but could feel jittery if characters are close together. Hello bubble pops in immediately.

C

Gaze Enter (hover-like)

Like a mouse hover — character reacts when your gaze enters its region and dismisses when you look away. Quick ~300ms delay to avoid flicker. Each character has a hitbox zone. Smooth fade in/out for the hello bubble.

Also: should the hello message be generic ("Hello! 👋") or contextual (e.g. "Hey! I'm working on the auth module")? The contextual version would use the session's current task description.
