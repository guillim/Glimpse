Making "Needs Input" Feel Urgent

3 approaches — each adds a distinct visual break from the calm idle/active states

A

Pulsing Orange Glow

A radiating glow ring pulses around the character, like a notification beacon. Other characters have no glow at all, so this is immediately noticeable.

Pros

  • Catches peripheral vision — works even when not looking directly
  • Subtle enough to not be annoying over long waits
  • Clean SpriteKit implementation (SKAction repeat + SKEffectNode glow)

Cons

  • If multiple agents ask at once, the scene could get "loud"
B

Bouncing + Red Badge

The character bounces up and down with a red "!" notification badge — like an app icon demanding attention. Maximum urgency.

Pros

  • Impossible to miss — motion + color + badge triple signal
  • Familiar pattern (iOS app badges, chat notifications)

Cons

  • Continuous bouncing can feel frantic/stressful after a few minutes
  • Breaks the calm aesthetic of the idle scene
C

Color Tint + Glow + Badge

The character's body shifts to warm orange, gains a pulsing glow, AND gets a "!" badge. No motion — the color break alone is striking since all other characters stay their natural hue. Three static-ish signals that stack.

  • Color shift is the strongest differentiator — no other state changes the character's hue
  • Less jittery than bouncing, but more visible than glow alone
  • Badge adds a familiar "unread" signal on top

Cons
